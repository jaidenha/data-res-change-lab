Your Mission Tonight

You are meeting Dr. Walker for dinner at Kaimana Terrace, a quiet uncrowded restaurant. Fabio had an urgent conflict and couldn't attend, but Jennifer assures you they've discussed the materials together.

What Jennifer Values

  • Efficiency & Brevity - She appreciates concise, clear communication. "Email is faster than phone calls."
  • Scientific Rigor - She asks sharp technical questions about coral genomics, DNA barcoding, and invasive species mitigation
  • Measurable Impact - She wants to see data, numbers, and long-term budget projections
  • Respect for Time - She's often between classes or meetings; values people who get to the point
  • Joint Decision-Making - All major gifts are decided with Fabio, though she often represents both

Personal Details to Remember

  • Interests: Aquatic life, conservation, sailing, being outdoors, surfing competitions (because of Fabio), genetic research
  • Dislikes: Crowded places, soda, irrelevant chatter, long-winded explanations, sunscreen scents (uses mineral-only)
  • Personality: Efficient, often distracted, highly selective with time, more personable in small relaxed settings
  • Travel: Visits Greece every couple years (minored in Greek, studied abroad there)

What NOT to Do

  • Don't mention Fabio's surfing clients - No relevance to the ask
  • Avoid too much personal reminiscing - Could irritate her; she expects purposeful conversation
  • Don't mention their rising wealth - Unnecessary and potentially offensive
  • Skip casual topics like their dog - Too informal for a major gift solicitation
  • Don't be long-winded - She values clarity and efficiency above all

Pro Tips for Success

  • Be direct and purposeful - she knows you're here for a specific ask
  • Lead with impact and science - connect to her professional expertise in genetics
  • Have specific numbers ready - budget, timelines, measurable outcomes
  • Acknowledge Fabio's input even though he's not present
  • Keep it conversational but professional - small groups work best with her
  • Reference the 3-year relationship naturally - show you've been listening
